The Terraform Provider for Zoom lets you manage Zoom Phone infrastructure as code. Declare your Zoom Phone configuration in HCL, and Terraform handles provisioning, updating, and tearing down resources through Zoom's API. The provider is built and maintained by [Folio Security](https://github.com/folio-sec) and is available on the Terraform Registry under the source address `folio-sec/zoom`. It is licensed under [Apache-2.0](https://github.com/folio-sec/terraform-provider-zoom/blob/main/LICENSE).

## What the provider manages

The provider covers the core Zoom Phone object model:

- **Sites** — `zoom_phone_site`: physical or logical locations that group phone users
- **Call queues** — `zoom_phone_call_queue`, `zoom_phone_call_queue_members`, `zoom_phone_call_queue_phone_numbers`, `zoom_phone_call_queue_policy_voice_mail`
- **Auto-receptionists** — `zoom_phone_auto_receptionist`, `zoom_phone_auto_receptionist_ivr`
- **Call handling** — `zoom_phone_call_handling_business_hours`, `zoom_phone_call_handling_closed_hours`, `zoom_phone_call_handling_holiday_hours`
- **Shared line groups** — `zoom_phone_shared_line_group`, `zoom_phone_shared_line_group_members`, `zoom_phone_shared_line_group_phone_numbers`
- **Users** — `zoom_phone_user`, `zoom_phone_user_calling_plans`, `zoom_phone_user_phone_numbers`
- **Blocked lists** — `zoom_phone_blocked_list`
- **External contacts** — `zoom_phone_external_contact`

Data sources are available for reading existing Zoom resources into your configuration, including `zoom_phone_site`, `zoom_phone_call_queue`, `zoom_phone_users`, and more.
